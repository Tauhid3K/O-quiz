About O'Quiz

O'Quiz is a simple and effective web-based platform for creating, managing, and taking quizzes online. Teachers and administrators can easily set up quizzes, and students get instant grading for objective questions.

Key Features

  • Create and manage quizzes easily.
  • Instant grading and results for students.
  • Supports both classroom and remote learning.
  • Future upgrades: more question types, leaderboards, mobile support, and analytics.

Our Goal

To streamline the quiz process, save time for educators, and provide a smooth learning experience for students.
